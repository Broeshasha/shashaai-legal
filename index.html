Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026

ShashaAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the ShashaAI Android application collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. Please read it carefully before using the app.

ShashaAI does not operate a backend server. It has no user accounts, no login system, and stores no personal data in any database owned by us. All intelligence is powered by the Google Gemini API using an API key you supply yourself.

1. Information We Collect

ShashaAI collects only the minimum data necessary to function:

  • Voice Audio: When you start a call, the app captures microphone audio in real time and streams it to Google's Gemini Live API for processing. This audio is not stored by ShashaAI on your device or on any server we control.
  • Your API Key: The Google AI Studio API key you enter is stored locally on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences system. It never leaves your device and is never sent to ShashaAI servers.
  • Conversation Summary: After each call, a brief text summary (approximately 3–5 sentences) of the conversation is generated and saved locally on your device in a private SQLite database. This is used to give the AI context in future calls. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Anonymous Session Analytics: When a call ends, the app sends a small anonymous payload to a Cloudflare serverless worker. This payload contains: (a) an anonymous device hash derived from a SHA-256 hash of a non-reversible system identifier — it cannot be used to identify you personally; (b) the duration of the call in seconds; (c) the call completion status; and (d) a timestamp. No audio, no name, no location, no personal identifiers are included.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Voice audio is used solely to generate a real-time AI response via Google Gemini. It is not used for any other purpose by ShashaAI.
  • Conversation summaries are used exclusively to personalise your experience across calls — so the AI remembers context from previous conversations.
  • Anonymous analytics are used by the developer to monitor app usage patterns (e.g., average call length, number of calls per day) for the sole purpose of improving the product.

3. Third-Party Services

ShashaAI relies on the following third-party services. Their respective privacy policies govern how they handle data passed to them:

  • Google Gemini API (Google LLC): Receives your voice audio for real-time AI processing. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Workers (Cloudflare, Inc.): Receives the anonymous analytics payload described above. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

ShashaAI does not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

4. Microphone Permission

ShashaAI requests the RECORD_AUDIO permission. This permission is used exclusively to capture your voice during AI calls. The microphone is active only while a call is in progress. The app does not access the microphone at any other time.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your API key is stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences, which encrypts data at rest using AES-256.
  • Conversation summaries are stored in a local SQLite database on your device, inaccessible to other apps.
  • No personal data is stored on any server controlled by ShashaAI.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (API key, conversation summaries) is deleted immediately when you uninstall the app. You can also clear all stored data at any time from within the app's settings screen. We have no server-side data to delete on your behalf, since none is stored by us.

7. Children's Privacy

ShashaAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of any data, contact us at support@kampus-link.com.
